About Us
Welcome to our radio station! We are a community-driven station that plays a variety of music, from top 40 hits to indie favorites. Our mission is to bring people together through the power of music and to create a sense of community and belonging. We have a team of dedicated DJs who handpick the songs you hear on air, and are committed to fostering local talent by regularly featuring music from up-and-coming artists in our area. We also produce and air our own original content, including daily news and weather updates, as well as local events and community-focused programs. Tune in to our live stream on our website, or download our mobile app to listen on the go. Want to request a song or talk to our DJs? Call or text us during our live shows, or send us a message on social media. Don't want to miss a beat? Follow us on Instagram, Twitter, and Facebook to stay up-to-date on all our events, giveaways and more. We are a commercial-free station, our revenue comes from our sponsors, who support our mission and vision. We believe that our sponsors share the same values as ours, supporting local communities and promoting diversity. We also have a wide range of podcasts, covering different topics such as music, politics, and entertainment. Our podcasts are produced by our team of experts and are available on our website and all major podcast platforms. We are committed to providing a diverse range of music and programming, catering to all ages and backgrounds. We understand that music is a powerful tool that can bring people together and create a sense of unity and belonging. We are proud to be a part of the community and to provide a platform for local talent and voices. So, whether you're at home, in the car, or at the gym, make us your go-to station for all your music needs. Thank you for listening, and we hope to be your soundtrack to your day!
